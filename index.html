The Crown Jewel of the North!

Donner's Bay Resort is located on the famous Turtle Flambeau Flowage in Iron County, Wisconsin.

From Fishing, to Hunting, Snowmobiling, or just plain relaxing - you've found the spot to stay!

Fishing

Bass, Crappie, Musky, Northern Pike, Perch, and Walleye are all available to the fisherman.

Hunting

Bear, Deer, Ducks, Grouse, and Rabbits are here for the hunter. Guides Available.

Snowmobile

Approx. 500 miles of groomed snowmobile trails in the area with one trail leaving the resort.

Camping

Plenty of campsites near the resort for those who want to brave the outdoors.

Donner's Bay offers the best Muskie fishing in Wisconsin

We have 6 cabins and campsites open year round for your enjoyment!

All Cabins include a 14' Lund Boat for the duration of your stay.
